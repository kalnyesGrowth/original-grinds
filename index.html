Our Story

More Than a Coffee Shop

Original Grinds is Fredericksburg's indie coffeeshop, vegan bakery, art gallery, and live music venue, all in one. Located on historic Caroline Street, OGs was built to be a creative safe space where good vibes and great music are always guaranteed.

Every drink is crafted with non-dairy ingredients. Every baked good is fully vegan. From lavender lattes to cheesy garlic rolls, the menu is built for everyone, whether you're vegan or just love good food and coffee.

The walls feature rotating art from local artists, available for purchase. Weekly events bring the community together for open mic nights, poetry readings, and live music. OGs is also a proud sober venue, providing a welcoming space for all.

FAQ

Questions & Answers

Is everything on the menu vegan?

Yes. Every drink and food item at Original Grinds is 100% vegan. All dairy alternatives are plant-based, and all baked goods are made without animal products.

Do you have WiFi and laptop-friendly seating?

We have free WiFi and comfortable seating for remote work. Multiple outlets are available. The space is great for working or studying.

What are your weekly events?

Open Mic Night happens every Wednesday, and Breaking Lines Poetry Night is every Friday. We also host live music throughout the week. Follow @ogsfxbg on Instagram for the latest schedule.

Can I bring my dog?

Yes, Original Grinds is pet friendly. Well-behaved dogs are welcome inside the shop.

Do you sell the art on the walls?

Yes. The art on display is created by local artists and is available for purchase. Ask at the counter for details on any piece that catches your eye.

Can I order for delivery?

Yes. Original Grinds is available on DoorDash and Seamless for delivery in the Fredericksburg area. You can also order ahead through the Joe Coffee app.

Where can I park?

Street parking is available on Caroline Street and surrounding blocks in downtown Fredericksburg. There are also public parking lots within walking distance.
